## Why AI Providers?

AI Providers is a configuration hub that simplifies AI integration in Obsidian. Instead of configuring AI settings separately in each plugin, you configure them once and share across all plugins that support AI Providers.

## Supported Providers

AI Providers supports 20+ AI providers out of the box:

- **OpenAI** - GPT-4, GPT-3.5, and embeddings
- **Anthropic** - Claude models with vision support
- **Ollama** - Local AI models for maximum privacy
- **Google Gemini** - Gemini Pro and Flash models
- **OpenRouter** - Access to multiple AI providers through one API
- **LM Studio** - Local model serving
- **Groq** - Fast inference for open source models
- And many more including Mistral AI, DeepSeek, xAI, Perplexity, and others
